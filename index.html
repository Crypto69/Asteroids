Controls

A reproduction of the original 1979 Atari Asteroids game, modified for accessible gaming with mouse-based controls.

Designed for players using a mouse, head mouse, gyroscopic pointer, or facial tracking.

Designed by Chris Venter.

How to play.

Move the mouse to aim the ship.

Left click fires.

Left hold thrusts.

Right click activates hyperspace.

Clear each wave by destroying every
asteroid and saucer.

Large asteroids split into two
medium asteroids.

Medium asteroids split into two
small asteroids.

Small asteroids are destroyed
completely.

Large asteroids score 20 points.

Medium asteroids score 50 points.

Small asteroids score 100 points.

Large saucers score 200 points.

Small saucers score 1000 points.

Saucer shots can break asteroids,
but only player shots score
asteroid points.

Earn an extra ship every 10000 points.

The score caps at 99990.

Hyperspace moves the ship to a
random location.

After losing a life, the ship
respawns away from touching
asteroids.

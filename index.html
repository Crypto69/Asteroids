Controls

A reproduction of the original 1979 Atari Asteroids game, modified for accessible gaming with mouse-based controls.

Designed for players using a mouse, head mouse, gyroscopic pointer, or facial tracking.

Designed by Chris Venter.

Move the mouse to aim the ship. Left click fires, left hold thrusts, and right click activates hyperspace.

Each wave begins with large asteroids drifting across a wraparound playfield. Anything leaving one edge returns from the opposite edge.

Destroy asteroids to clear each wave. Large asteroids split into two medium rocks, medium rocks split into two small rocks, and small rocks are destroyed.

Scoring: large asteroids 20 points, medium asteroids 50 points, small asteroids 100 points.

Large saucers fire inaccurately and score 200 points. Small saucers aim more precisely as your score rises and score 1000 points.

Saucer shots can break asteroids, but only player shots earn asteroid points. Earn an extra ship every 10000 points. The score caps at 99990.

Hyperspace moves the ship to a random location with a cooldown. After losing a life, the ship respawns away from touching asteroids.
